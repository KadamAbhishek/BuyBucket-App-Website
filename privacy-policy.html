Privacy Policy for Buy Bucket App

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Buy Bucket App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Buy Bucket mobile application (the "App").

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

The Buy Bucket App is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. The vast majority of your data remains strictly on your local device. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Local App Data: All your inventory items, shopping lists, pantry records, budget settings, custom categories, and personal preferences (such as App Theme and Default Currency) are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain remote servers or databases to secretly collect this data.
  • Account Information: If you choose to enter a "User Name" or "Email" within the Settings screen, this information is stored locally on your device to personalize your app experience.
  • Clipboard Access: The App requests access to your device's clipboard solely when you tap the "Import from Clipboard" button to paste a shared shopping list. We do not read or store clipboard data in the background.

3. Google Drive Integration & Cloud Backups

To prevent data loss, Buy Bucket offers a feature to securely back up and restore your app data using your personal Google Drive account.

  • Authentication: The App uses Google Sign-In to authenticate your account securely. We do not see or store your Google password.
  • Data Access: If you enable Google Drive Backup, the App requests permission to create, read, and overwrite a single, specific backup file (my_buy_list_backup.json) within your Google Drive.
  • Strict Isolation: The Buy Bucket App cannot view, read, or modify any other files, photos, or documents in your Google Drive. It only accesses the specific backup file it creates.

4. Third-Party Services and Links

The App contains features that interact with third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Online Search Links: The App provides convenient buttons to search for items directly on platforms like Amazon and Flipkart. Tapping these buttons securely formats a search URL and opens it in your native browser or the respective native app.

We do not share your shopping lists or data with these platforms. However, once you leave the Buy Bucket App or are redirected to a third-party website, your activity is governed by their respective Privacy Policies. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these external sites.

5. Security of Your Data

We value your trust in providing us with your personal data and use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data relies heavily on your device's security. We highly recommend using a screen lock (PIN, pattern, or biometric) on your mobile device to protect your Buy Bucket data from unauthorized physical access.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Buy Bucket App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's features or legal requirements. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy or data practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
