🛒 BuyBucket: Complete Feature Guide

Welcome to BuyBucket — your all-in-one smart shopping list, budget tracker, and pantry manager. Whether you are tracking daily groceries, planning a massive trekking trip, or keeping an eye on your monthly spending, BuyBucket is designed to keep you organized.

Below is a complete guide to everything BuyBucket can do to make your shopping experience seamless.

📝 1. Smart Inventory Management

Never forget an item again. BuyBucket keeps your wishlist organized and prioritizes what you need to buy first.

  • Add Detailed Items: Add items with specific details including Name, Category, Priority (High, Medium, Low), Expected Buy Date, and Estimated Price.
  • Pending vs. Purchased: Items live in your "Pending" list until you buy them. Once purchased, simply tap "Mark Bought," enter the actual price paid, and it moves to your "Purchased" history.
  • Smart Sorting: BuyBucket automatically sorts your "Pending" list to show the items you need to buy soonest right at the top. Your "Purchased" list is sorted to show your most recent buys first.
  • Custom Categories: Organize items your way. Create custom categories (e.g., "Camping Gear," "Office Supplies") to group your items perfectly.

📊 2. Financial Dashboard & Analytics

Take total control of your budget. BuyBucket gives you real-time insights into exactly where your money is going.

  • Dual-Layer Budget Tracking: The Dashboard features an intuitive progress bar. The solid green bar shows what you have actually spent, while the lighter background bar projects your expected spending. You will always know if you are safely within budget or projected to overspend.
  • Separate Grocery & Item Budgets: Track your recurring grocery bills completely separate from your individual bucket list items.
  • Visual Analytics: Tap on any budget card to dive into the Spending Report. View beautiful Pie Charts that break down your spending by category.
  • Time-Travel History: Use the Month Navigator to look back up to 6 months into the past and compare your spending habits over time.

🥦 3. Smart Pantry Tracker

Stop throwing away expired food. The Smart Pantry tracks your perishable goods and alerts you before they go bad.

  • Seamless Transfer: When you buy groceries, toggle "Send to Pantry" and assign an expiry date.
  • Color-Coded Alerts:
    🟢 Teal (Safe): Plenty of time left.
    🟠 Orange (Expiring Soon): Expires in less than 3 days.
    🔴 Red (Expired): The item has passed its expiry date.
  • Quick Consume: Used it up? Simply swipe right on the item in your Pantry tab and tap "Consume" to clear it from your inventory.

📋 4. Multi-List (Groceries) Management

Planning a weekend trip or a monthly grocery haul? Create custom lists to manage bulk purchases.

  • Checklists on the Go: Create dedicated lists (e.g., "March Groceries" or "Party Supplies"). Check items off as you walk through the store aisles.
  • Log Total Bills: Once a list is fully checked off, hit "Complete List" and log the total receipt amount to instantly update your dashboard budget.
  • Duplicate Lists: Buying the same things next month? Swipe and tap "Duplicate" to instantly recreate your old list and start fresh.
  • One-Tap WhatsApp Sharing: Tap the share icon to instantly generate a cleanly formatted, numbered text list and send it to your family or friends via WhatsApp, Email, or SMS.

🛍️ 5. One-Tap Online Shopping

Found something you want to buy, but want to check prices online first?

  • Direct Integrations: Open any pending item and tap the branded Amazon or Flipkart buttons.
  • Skip the Typing: BuyBucket will instantly open the respective shopping app on your phone and automatically search for the exact name of your item. Find it, buy it, and check it off!

☁️ 6. Secure Data Backup & Restore

Your data is yours. Keep your shopping history, pantry items, and budgets safely backed up.

  • Google Drive Sync: Securely link your Google account to backup all your data to the cloud. Restore it instantly if you get a new phone or reinstall the app.
  • Local Device Export: Prefer to keep things offline? Generate a .json backup file directly to your phone's local storage. You can easily share this file via Email or Drive, and import it later.

⚙️ 7. Customization & App Preferences

Make BuyBucket feel like your app.

  • In-App Reminders: Set a specific time (e.g., 9:00 AM) to receive daily push notifications reminding you of items due today or pantry foods expiring soon.
  • Global Currency Support: Choose your preferred default currency (₹, $, €, £, ¥) from the settings, and all your dashboard metrics will update instantly.
  • App Themes: Seamlessly switch between Light Theme, Dark Theme, or simply follow your device's System Default.
  • Over-The-Air Updates: BuyBucket features a smart internal update checker. Whenever a new version is released with bug fixes or features, a prompt will appear letting you download the latest update directly from the official website.

Ready to simplify your shopping?

Download BuyBucket today and take the stress out of managing your wishlist, your groceries, and your budget!
